How will we charge the clients?

We will charge the clients on a Kilometer basis, meaning for every kilometer completed from the start point to the drop off point. (min 1.5 KWD) * *

How to add F.O.D. Tokens to your account?

User will have to recharge their FOD accounts with FOD Tokens, which will be used to pay for the delivery services. This can easily be done through any secure and recognized payment platform such as KNET, Visa, etc. The Tokens will be then transferred to the customers account immediately after the payment confirmation is made.
